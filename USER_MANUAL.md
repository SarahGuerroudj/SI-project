# RouteMind User Manual

Welcome to the RouteMind User Manual. This guide will help you navigate the system based on your assigned role.

## Getting Started

### Login & Registration
- **Access**: The platform is URL-accessible. On first visit, you will be prompted with a login modal.
- **Registration**: If you are a new client, use the "Sign Up" tab to create an account using your email.
- **Google Sign-In**: You can use your Google workspace account for a one-click login experience.

Admins have the highest level of authority and are responsible for system governance and security.

### User & Role Management
- **Full Control**: Admins can Create, Read, Update, and Delete any user in the system.
- **Role Assignment**: Specifically, admins can promote users to "Manager" or "Driver" roles.
- **Create Managers**: Use the administrative dashboard to onboard new managers into the system.

### System Monitoring
- **Audit Logs**: Access the **Audit Logs** section to track all critical actions, including:
    - Successful and failed login attempts.
    - Data modifications (creating/deleting clients, shipments, etc.).
    - Role and permission changes.
- **Reporting**: Filter logs by severity (High, Medium, Low) to identify potential security risks or operational bottlenecks.

## Manager Guide

As a Manager, you have full control over the logistics operations.

### Client Management
- Navigate to **Clients** in the sidebar.
- **Add Client**: Click "Add Client" to register a new business partner.
- **Quick Links**: View client details, billing history, and active shipments directly from the list.

### Fleet & Drivers
- **Fleet**: Manage your vehicles and their maintenance status.
- **Drivers**: Add new drivers and verify their current status (Available, On Route, Off Duty).

### Destinations & Pricing
- **Destinations**: Configure warehouses, hubs, and checkpoints.
- **Pricing Rules**: Set custom base prices and per-km rates for different service types.

---

## Driver Guide

Drivers use a simplified dashboard optimized for mobile and tablet use.

### Managing Routes
- **Active Routes**: On your dashboard, you will see routes assigned to you for today.
- **Complete Delivery**: When a delivery is finished, click "Complete Delivery".
- **Logging**: Enter actual distance traveled and fuel consumed to keep the fleet data accurate.

---

## Client Guide

Clients can track their shipments in real-time.

### Shipments
- **Create Shipment**: Click "New Shipment" to request a delivery.
- **Tracking**: Monitor the status of your shipments (Pending, In Transit, Delivered).
- **Invoices**: View and download PDF invoices for completed services under the **Billing** section.

---

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

**Q: I added a client but they don't appear in the list.**
A: Ensure you filled all required fields. The list refreshes automatically, but you can try a manual refresh if the connection is slow.

**Q: How do I change my profile details?**
A: Navigate to the **Account** section at the bottom of the sidebar to update your phone number and bio.

**Q: Google Sign-In is failing.**
A: Ensure you are using the email address registered with the system or that you have a stable internet connection.
